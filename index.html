Thesis Project

E-Commerce Recommender System Using Association Rule

  • The dataset was collected from TensorFlow with the focus on Home & Kitchen and Health & Personal categories

  • Rework to implement a scalable Association Rule algorithm for a large dataset

  • Tested with a small artificial dataset before applying to the data of interest

  • The result suggests firms to differentiate their products and recommend to customers goods with similar usage which can complement each other.

    Project 1

    Predicting Fraud Detection in Online Transactions using Data Mining techniques

  • The dataset was collected from Kaggle's IEEE-CIS Fraud Detection Contest

  • Statistically analyzed and visualized possible factors correlated to fraud commitment

  • Used Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, Neural Network to build and K-fold Cross Validation to evaluate fraud detection models

  • Measures of model accuracy include Average Accuracy, Average Precision, Macro F-1 score

  • All models resulted in high Average Accuracy but Neural Network yielded the best Macro F-1 score

    Project 2

    The Visual Story of Music Trend for the past 40 years

  • This project is the visualization to analyze the top trendy songs of each decade since 1980s.

  • The original dataset was obtained from Kaggle contains over 600,000 songs with their track ID, audio features, artist, and realease date since 1921.

  • A subset of each decade's top 200 songs were selected to represent the trendy music during their period.

  • Features such as Danceability and Speechiness increased over 40 years while Energy, Valence, and Tempo do not show clear tendencies.

    Project 3

    The Impact of Economic Factors on Economic Growth

  • Data from Penn World Table and World Bank since 2011 were combined to create a more detailed dataset

  • I am especially interested in analyzing how human capital influence economic growth

  • Other factors being studied are FDI, Regions, and Income Group (low/high income countries, etc.)

  • The effect of the explanatory variables are studied by using multiple linear regression backed with stepwise regression to finalize the model.

  • FDI and human capital have positive effect on economy. Lower-middle income countries are experiencing the fastest growing rate among other groups to catch up with the world economy.
